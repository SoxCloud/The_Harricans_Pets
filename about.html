About Us

At The Hurricans Pets, we live and breathe aquatic life. What started as a passion project by our lead specialist Mr. Ian —a fishkeeping veteran with 20+ years of experience—has grown into Johannesburg’s go-to destination for everything fish-related. From beginner hobbyists to seasoned breeders and fish service providers, we’re here to make your underwater journey effortless and joyful!
